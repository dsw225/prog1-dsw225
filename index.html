Biography

Early Life:
Born on July 31, 1980, Harry James Potter's early years were marred by tragedy when his parents were murdered by Lord Voldemort. Orphaned and mistreated by his Aunt and Uncle, Harry's life took a magical turn on his eleventh birthday when he discovered his wizarding heritage.

Arrival at Hogwarts:
A natural talent in Quidditch and Defense Against the Dark Arts, he faced numerous challenges, including encounters with magical creatures and uncovering mysteries like the Sorcerer's Stone. As the Boy Who Lived, Harry played a pivotal role in defeating Voldemort, cementing his legacy as a symbol of courage in the wizarding world.
